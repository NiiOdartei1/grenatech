About GreNaTech

GreNaTech combines technology, community knowledge and long-term commitment to deliver practical, locally-driven solutions for climate resilience and sustainable livelihoods. We design renewable energy systems, climate-smart agriculture, hands-on education, and circular economy initiatives that uplift communities and protect ecosystems.

Who We Are

We are a cross-disciplinary collective of environmentalists, engineers and educators who believe technology can be a force for environmental justice. Our projects blend modern tools with local knowledge to create scalable solutions — from solar-powered infrastructure to climate education and regenerative farming.

Inclusivity is central to our work: we prioritize participatory approaches, gender equity, and long-term partnerships with communities, governments and institutions to ensure solutions are locally owned and sustained.

Our Mission

To empower communities by designing and deploying climate-resilient technologies and educational programs that create dignified livelihoods, restore ecosystems, and reduce vulnerability to climate change.

Our Vision

A world where technology and nature work together — farmers, communities and cities thriving through resilient systems that are equitable, regenerative and scalable.

What We Do
  • Solar and renewable energy installations for schools and clinics
  • Climate education programs for youth and community leaders
  • Reforestation, permaculture and regenerative agriculture
  • Tech innovation for water, waste and smallholder productivity
